Classification

Typefaces are broken down into a variety of classifications based on their characteristics. These differences can be subtle or fairly obvious. Some typefaces are better suited as headlines, while other are better suited for body text.

Using different typefaces helps bring meaning and understanding to our designs. In cases where typography is used correctly, users can identify and understand our designs much more clearly and easily. This is an essential part of good design.

Legibility is how well we can see the letters of a specific typeface.

Readability is how easy we can read can read the copy.

Vertical Rhythm

Just like lines on a piece of notebook paper, vertical rhythm helps us stay organized by using evenly spaced horizontal lines as a guide for our markup. There are a few good tools to check your vertical rhythm.

Our vertical rhythm is influenced by the size of our font, margin and line-height. A great tool that easily allows us to calculate our font sizes is The Modular Scale. After our font sizes are figured out, it's time to calculate our margin and line-height.
