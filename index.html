A font instructs the computer how to present or print the typeface.

A typeface is the shape of the letters, numbers, and symbols that make up its overall design.

Typeface Classifications

Typefaces are classified based on certain characteristics. The differences between typefaces may be subtle or fairly obvious. Some typefaces are better suited as headlines, while others are better suited for body text.

Vertical Rhythm

Just like lines on a piece of notebook paper, vertical rhythm helps us stay organized by using evenly spaced horizontal lines as a guide for our pages. Our vertical rhythm is influenced by the size of our font, margin and line-height.

There are a few great tools that can be used to achieve good vertical rhythm. The Modular Scale helps us calculate our font sizes and margins. Basehold.it provides intructions on how to add a customizable background image that visually allows us to check our vertical rhythm.
