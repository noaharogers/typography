Legibility

&

Readability

Using typography in such a way that users can easily identify and understand what we are trying to communicate is essential for good design.

Legibility refers to the design of the actual typeface.

Readability refers to how we use our typography to make it easier to read.

Vertical Rhythm

Just like lines on a peice of notebook paper, vertical rhythm helps us stay organized by using a set of evenly spaced vertical lines on our projects. There are a few good tools to check your vertical rhythm. My personal favorite is basehold.it.

The vertical rhythm on your page is influenced not only by the size of your font, but also the line height, margin and, padding. One of the best way to determine our exact sizes is to use The Modular Scale.
